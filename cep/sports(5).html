AAI Sports Scholarship Scheme

Eligibility Criteria:

• Athletes aged between 14 and 24 years.
• Applicants must have participated in sub-junior / junior / senior national championships, All India Inter University tournaments, or national school games from 2021–22 onwards.

Required Document:

• Age proof (birth certificate / school leaving certificate / municipal birth record / passport)
• Aadhaar card (or equivalent identity proof)
• Family income proof (ITR, income certificate from competent authority, or salary slips)
• Passport‑size photograph
• Sports participation / achievement certificates (state / national / international)

Application guidance

Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
Step 1 = Go to the Scholarship Page
➤ Visit https://www.buddy4study.com
➤ Search and click on AAI Sports Scholarship Scheme
Step 2 = Login or Register
➤ Login using email/mobile
➤ Or register a new account if you're new
Step 3 = Click “Apply Now”
➤ Opens the scholarship application form
Step 4 = Fill Basic Details
➤ Name, DOB, gender, contact info, ID proof
Step 5 = Fill Educational Details
➤ Current class/course, school/college, marks
Step 6 = Enter Family & Income Details
➤ Parent's occupation, annual income
➤ Upload income certificate / ITR
Step 7 = Enter Sports Achievements
➤ Sport name, level (state/national), year
➤ Upload certificates (PDF/image)
Step 8 = Upload Required Documents
➤ Photo, Aadhaar, sports certs, income proof, etc.
Step 9 = Review Your Form
➤ Double-check all info and uploads
Step 10 = Submit the Application
➤ Click “Submit”
➤ Save confirmation or screenshot
