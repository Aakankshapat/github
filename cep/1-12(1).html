    Maharashtra Pre-Matric Scholarship (SC, Classes 5-10) Government of Maharashtra

    Provides financial assistance to students from Scheduled Caste (SC) families studying in classes 5 to 10 in Maharashtra.

      Eligibility Criteria:

      •Belong to Scheduled Caste (SC)
      •Family belongs to Below Poverty Line (BPL)
      •Must have passed previous year; attendance requirement etc.

      Requried Document:

      • Income Certificate
      • Caste Certificate
      • BPL Card
      • Passport-size photograph
      • School enrollment proof etc.

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      Step 1: Click on the 'Apply Now' button below.
      Step 2: Click on the 'Register' button and fill in the required registration details. (Note: If already registered, log in using Gmail/Mobile number/Email ID).
      Step 3: After successful registration on the portal, click on the 'Applicant login' button on the right-hand side of the dashboard.
      Step 4: Log in using the username and password, and select the respective scholarship.
      Step 5: Fill in the necessary details, upload the required documents, and submit.
