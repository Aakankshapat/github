Khelo India Scholarship (National Level)

Eligibility Criteria:

•Must be an Indian citizen.
•Age: Usually for school students (approx. 10–18 years).
•Should have participated or won in district / state / national level competitions.
•Recommended by a recognized sports federation / school / state sports department

Required Document:

•Aadhaar card or other ID proof.
•Date of birth proof (birth certificate or school record).
•Sports certificates (district/state/national level).
•School ID or bonafide certificate.
•Bank account details (in student’s name).
•Passport size photographs.
•Medical / fitness certificate (if asked).

Application guidance

Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
Step 1 = Visit official portal → kheloindia.gov.in
Step 2 = Register as an athlete (create login ID).
Step 3 = Fill the online form with personal, academic, and sports details.
Step 4 = Upload scanned documents and certificates.
Step 5 = Submit and take a printout for reference.
Step 6 = Wait for verification and selection notice by Khelo India officials.
