    Technical Education Scholarship Scheme for Degree Level (TESSDL)

      Eligibility Criteria:

      •Students doing full-time degree / technical education.

      Requried Document:

      • Admission / enrollment proof.
      • Fee payment receipts.
      • Income Certificate.
      • Identity proof (Aadhaar, etc.).
      • Bank account details (with IFSC, etc.).
      • Grade / marksheet of qualifying exam.

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      step-1: Visit the official scheme page on MyScheme portal.
      step-2: Read eligibility and instructions.
      step-3: Register or login.
      step-4: Fill in required personal, academic, income details.
      step-5: Upload documents.
      step-6: Submit application.
      step-7: Monitor status.

      Official link of the AICTE Students Development Schemes
