Rajarshri Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship for VJNT & SBC students Government of Maharashtra

      Eligibility Criteria:

      •Must belong to VJNT or SBC category.
      •Studying in 11th or 12th standard.
      •Scored 75% or above in SSC (10th).
      •Must be a Maharashtra domicile.
      •No income limit.

      Required Document:

      •Caste Certificate (VJNT/SBC)
      •10th Marksheet (75%+)
      •Domicile Certificate
      •Aadhaar Card
      •Admission Proof (11th/12th)
      •Bank Passbook
      •Transfer Certificate (TC)

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      Step 1 = Go to: https://mahadbtmahait.gov.in
      Step 2 = Register/Login using Aadhaar & mobile.
      Step 3 = Complete profile (personal, bank, education).
      Step 4 = Select Social Justice Dept → Apply to this scholarship.
      Step 5 = Upload documents & submit.
      Step 6 = Track status under “My Applications”
