Post‑Matric Scholarship for Students with Disabilities (Maharashtra)

Eligibility Criteria:

•Medical students with ≥40% disability
• Admitted to government / recognized colleges

Required Document:

•Disability certificate
•Income certificate
•Domicile certificate
•School admission proof / bonafide
•Bank passbook

Application guidance

Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
Step 1 = Visit the central scholarship portal (e.g. scholarship.gov.in) for Pre‑Matric schemes.
Step 2 = Under “Student Login / Application”, register (or login) with credentials.
Step 3 = Fill personal details, school / class, disability details, bank details etc.
Step 4 = Upload required documents (scans).
Step 5 = Submit the application before deadline.
Step 6 = The application is verified by the school / district authorities.
Step 7 = Once approved, the scholarship amount is transferred (DBT) to your bank account
