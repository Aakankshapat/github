Jyoti Prakash Scholarship for Sportspersons

Eligibility Criteria:

• Must be an Indian citizen.
• Age between 14 and 25 years as on cut‑off date.
• Must have represented at state / national / international levels in sport(s) in the past 3 years.
• Annual family income must be less than ₹6,00,000.
• Must be pursuing education (school, college, or post‑graduate) in the relevant period.

Required Document:

• Age proof (birth certificate / school leaving certificate / municipal birth record / passport)
• Aadhaar card (or equivalent identity proof)
• Family income proof (ITR, income certificate from competent authority, or salary slips)
• Passport‑size photograph
• Sports participation / achievement certificates (state / national / international)
