Rajarshri Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship (for SC/OBC/VJNT/SBC) Government of Maharashtra

      Eligibility Criteria:

      •Belong to Scheduled Caste (SC/OBC/VJNT/SBC)
      •Annual family income should be up to ₹8 lakh (varies by category)

      Required Document:

      •Caste Certificate
      •income certificate
      •Admission receipt
      •Domicile Certificate
      •Bank Passbook (Xerox)

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      Step 1 = Go to: https://mahadbt.maharashtra.gov.in
      Step 2 = Register with Aadhaar & mobile number
      Step 3 = Complete profile
      Step 4 = Go to "Apply for Scholarship"
      Step 5 = Select: "Rajarshri Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Merit Scholarship"
      Step 6 = Fill details, upload documents
      Step 7 = Submit & track status
