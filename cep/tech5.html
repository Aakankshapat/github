    Foundation for Excellence (FFE) Scholarship

      Eligibility Criteria:

      • Must be academically meritorious.
      • Must be from economically disadvantaged background.
      • Must have secured admission in eligible professional courses.

      Requried Document:

      • Admission / offer letter.
      • Academic records / mark sheets.
      • Proof of family income.
      • Identity proof.
      • Bank details.
      • Any other documents requested by FFE (e.g. proof of hardship).

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      step-1: Visit FFE website: https://ffe.org/scholarships/
      step-2: Find the scholarship program applicable to your course.
      step-3: Register and fill application form.
      step-4:Upload required documents.
      step-5: Submit application.
      step-6: The foundation does verification, selection, and then disbursement.
