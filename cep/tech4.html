    Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship

      Eligibility Criteria:

      • Must be a student of India.
      • Family income limit (as per scheme) — for engineering, the scheme mentions certain supports. Education Ministry of India
      • Admission in recognized courses / colleges.
      • Fulfill other NSP criteria (submit application via NSP).

      Requried Document:

      • fee receipts.
      • Admission / enrollment proof.
      • marksheet of qualifying class.
      • Income Certificate.
      • Identity proof (Aadhaar, etc.).
      • Bank account details (with IFSC, etc.).

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      step-1: Go to the National Scholarship Portal (NSP).
      step-2: Register via One Time Registration (OTR) using Aadhaar or enrolment ID.
      step-3: Login with credentials
      step-4: Find “Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship” among schemes
      step-5: Fill application form, upload documents.
      step-6: Submit.
      step-7: Track status via the portal.
