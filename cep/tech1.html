    AICTE Pragati Scholarship Scheme

      Eligibility Criteria:

      •Female student.
      •Family income below a certain threshold (often ₹6 lakhs per year)
      •Admitted in first year (fresh) or renewal in later year if scholarship was already granted.
      •Must be studying in an AICTE-approved institution.

      Benefit / scholarship amount:

      Typically the scheme gives support toward tuition fees plus a contingency allowance (e.g. ₹30,000 toward tuition + ₹2,000/month for 10 months) (this figure is indicative

      Requried Document:

      • Admission / enrollment proof.
      • Fee payment receipts.
      • Income Certificate.
      • Identity proof (Aadhaar, etc.).
      • Bank account details (with IFSC, etc.).
      • Grade / marksheet of qualifying exam.
      • Passport size photograph.

      Application guidance

      Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
      step-1: Visit the AICTE “Students Development Schemes” page.
      step-2. Check the call for Pragati Scholarship and open the application link.
      step-3. Register / login with your credentials.
      step-4. Fill the application form with personal, academic, and family income details.
      step-5. Upload required documents.
      step-6. Submit and note the application number / acknowledgment.
      step-7. Track status on the portal.
