National Scholarship Portal (NSP), Government of India

Eligibility Criteria:

•Must be Indian citizen
•Studying in a recognized school / college / institute in India
•Minimum marks in previous qualifying exam (often 50%) — depends on scheme
•Annual family income below prescribed limit (varies per scheme)
•Other specific conditions depending on the particular scholarship (e.g. category, course, domicile)

Required Document:

•Aadhaar card / Aadhaar Enrolment ID
•Income Certificate (by competent authority)
•Caste Certificate (if applicable)
•Educational certificates / mark sheets of previous exams
•Admission proof / fee receipt / enrollment certificate
•Bank account details (passbook / account number, IFSC)
•Recent passport size photograph
•Domicile / residential certificate (if required)
•Other certificates (e.g. disability certificate, migration, etc.) if the scheme demands

Application guidance

Eligible applicants can apply by following these steps:-
Step 1 = Go to NSP portal → scholarships.gov.in
Step 2 = One-Time Registration (OTR): Register using Aadhaar/Aadhaar Enrolment ID to generate OTR / Reference number.
Step 3 = Face / Aadhaar authentication (if required by the portal)
Step 4 = Login using OTR / reference number
Step 5 = Select the applicable scholarship scheme
Step 6 = Fill personal, academic, bank & other required details
Step 7 = Upload the required documents (scanned)
Step 8 = Submit application
Step 9 = Institute / school verifies your application
Step 10 = Final approval & disbursement via DBT / PFMS
