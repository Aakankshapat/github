ONGC Sports Scholarship (India, National)

Eligibility Criteria:

• Indian athletes (of age approx 14 to 25 years; for certain sports lower minimum age)
• Must not be receiving another scholarship or stipend concurrently (they often ask for an undertaking).
• Performance at national / international (or state) levels as per scheme norms.

Required Document:

• Age proof (birth certificate / school leaving certificate / municipal birth record / passport)
• Aadhaar card (or equivalent identity proof)
• Family income proof (ITR, income certificate from competent authority, or salary slips)
• Passport‑size photograph
• Sports participation / achievement certificates (state / national / international)
